About the Authoress

Miriam Oglesby is a poet, technologist, and storyteller. A lifelong learner and feminist, she finds inspiration in human connection and the complexities of emotion. With endless gratitude to her midwife for sparking the idea, Miriam turned to writing as her refuge after miscarriage, processing and transforming grief into poetry. Carrying You Always is her debut collection.
